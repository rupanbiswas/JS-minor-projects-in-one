How To help ?

Voluteer

You can also volunteer to participate in campaigns organized by us or help us in fundraising events.The feeling of giving , and having the ability to touch lives is very empowering and satisfying.

Donation

There can never be enough to fund the necessities of the beneficiaries. We have been able to come a long way because of our generous givers. You can help financially by donating through our website and also set up a recurring monthly donation. You can also send cheques through mail or donate directly to the organization.

Share

Donate your old clothes, blankets, your children’s toys and books to the people who need it. If there is excess food left from any event, instead of throwing it away, you can share it with the hungry and the needy.

Save The Earth

Let us not ignore our responsibilities towards nature. Plant trees and make this earth green for us and for generations to come.
