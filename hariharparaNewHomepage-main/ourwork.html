Our Works

PLANT TREES

Hariharpara Gram Kalyan Society plants trees two times in a year. We also try to create awareness regarding the importance of trees for our well being. We educate the local people about how the trees help in combating global warming, decreasing the temperature of a place, preventing floods and soil erosion and acts as a barrier against the ultra violet rays.

LATRINES

We work alongside the Block Development Office to fulfill the basic hygiene requirements of the people by building latrines in the underprivileged areas . Our mission is to provide each house with a toilet and we are working tirelessly towards it.

PANDEMIC RELIEF

During the Covid 19 crisis, we provided food packages to 150 families. Each package consisted of 5kgs of rice, 1kg of lentils and 1 kg of oil. The families which had babies, received additional baby foods. Foods were supplied to the needy, every single day for 3 months.

We also have caring center for old aged people and conduct various workshops for other countless issues like women development, dalit upliftment, drinking water, mico finance , health and family welfare, rural development and poverty alleviation,youth affairs etc.
