Bottle Koozie Fabrics

TA koozie is used to insulate a chilled beverage from warming by warm air or sunlight. Using a koozie can reduce the rate a drink warms in the sun by up to 50% Materials like foam, neoprene, and polyurethane are most popular for KOOZIES

  • Properties :

  • The material is an excellent insulator
  • Resistance to Water, Oil & Grease
  • Wide Resiliency Rangee
  • Strong Bonding Properties
  • Performance in Harsh Environments
  • Mold, Mildew & Fungus Resistance

Support Fabrics

Support Fabrics are used to give shape and to provide support. Support fabric is used in openings, buttonholes, cuffs collars where fabric is strained and many more. These Fabrics serve several purposes which vary with the fabric of the garment and with the nature of the pattern design

  • Properties :

  • To control stretch in the entire garment
  • To add extra body to the fabric of the entire garment for additional weighte
  • To add extra body or stiffness to certain portions
  • To act as a cushion for bulky seams.
