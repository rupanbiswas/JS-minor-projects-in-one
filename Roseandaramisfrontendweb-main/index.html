When we begin to customize your bouquet for your loved ones, we first choose the colour scheme, the shape of the arrangement for eg.dome,triangular, or an asymmetrical cluster and then the flowers that go well together.
The stems are then trimmed diagonally at the bottom. The flowers need to have a sturdy support or they will fall apart. The base for the kind of flower arrangement required is chosen.Whether you require short flower arrangement or long luscious bouquet, the correct foundation is selected, be it a sponge box or a basket.
The greens for eg ivy and eucalyptus are added as a background after their stems are trimmed. Ribbon or extra accessories required by the customer are added. Fresh water is sprayed on the flowers to keep them hydrated.
